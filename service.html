Our Services

Bokamoso Science and Technology Education Centre (BOSTEC) delivers a wide range of science and technology education services tailored to learners, educators, and communities across South Africa. Our programmes are designed to uplift, educate, and empower.

🎓 Educator Development

  • 📘 Conduct educator workshops in Mathematics and Science
  • 🤝 Provide classroom support to teachers in STEM subjects
  • 🧪 Facilitate educator forums for learning and information sharing

🔬 Science Exhibit Centre

  • 🧬 Provide laboratory offerings in Physical Science, Life Sciences, and Natural Sciences
  • 🎭 Host engaging science shows to spark curiosity and public interest

  • 📝 Provide Grade 12 examination support lessons
  • 🎓 Host camps for high-potential learners to boost performance in Maths & Science (Train-a-Trainer Programme)

  • 💼 Deliver career development programmes focused on STEM careers
  • 🎯 Guide learners in making informed choices about science-related professions

🧑‍🏫 Teacher Development Workshops

Our Teacher Development Workshops are designed to strengthen classroom practice, boost confidence, and promote excellence in STEM education. These sessions bring together educators from diverse backgrounds to share insights, explore new methodologies, and deepen their subject knowledge.

  • 📘 Hands-on training in Mathematics, Physical Science, and Life Sciences
  • 🧠 Focus on inquiry-based learning and practical classroom strategies
  • 🤝 Peer-to-peer collaboration and mentorship opportunities
  • 📊 Integration of technology and digital tools in teaching
  • 🎓 CPD-aligned content to support professional growth

Educators engaging in hands-on science activities during a BOSTEC development workshop.

🎉 Events & Community Engagement

BOSTEC hosts a variety of science and technology events throughout the year to inspire learners, empower educators, and engage communities. These events celebrate innovation, heritage, and the power of education.

  • 📅 National Science Week — Interactive exhibitions, science shows, and outreach activities aligned with national themes.
  • 🎭 Heritage Day Celebrations — A fusion of culture and technology showcasing traditional knowledge and modern innovation.
  • 🚀 STEM Career Expo — Connecting learners with professionals, universities, and career pathways in science and engineering.
  • 🧪 Science Festivals — Hands-on experiments, competitions, and public demonstrations to spark curiosity and excitement.
  • 🏕️ Holiday Camps — Enrichment programmes during school breaks focused on Maths, Science, and leadership development.

These events are open to learners, educators, parents, and community members. They reflect our commitment to inclusive, impactful science education.
